Frequently Asked Questions

What is this system used for?
This system is designed to help students and staff report and manage maintenance issues within university residences efficiently.
How do I report a maintenance fault?
Simply click the "Create Ticket" option in your left side bar and fill out the necessary details on the "Ticket Creation Page".
Can I track the status of my ticket request?
Yes, you can track its status from "Ticket Requests" on your dashboard to see if it's been acknowledged, scheduled, or resolved.
What type of maintenance fault can I report?
You can report any issue related to the upkeep and maintenance of your residence, including plumbing, electrical, or general repairs.
What should I do if I forget my password?
You can reset your password from your Profile page. Click on "Settings" or the profile icon to navigate there.
